WELCOME TO MIET

The Manoharbhai Patel Institute of Engineering & Technology covers 60 acres of land. Constructions of about 1 Lakhs Sq. Ft. comprising of well equipped laboratories, classrooms, workshops, centralized as well as departmental level Computer Centers, Library, Training and Placement Cell, etc. within the campus. MIET has emerged as a one of the premier institute in Central India. The Institute was established in the year 2010. It is considered one of the premier engineering institutes of this region. The Institute is affiliated to R.T.M. Nagpur University and is approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. The infrastructure is second to none-well stocked library, well equipped workshop, state-of-art computer centers, drawing studios, lecture halls, laboratories, conference hall, seminar hall, etc.
